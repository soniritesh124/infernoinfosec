About Us

INFERNO INFOSEC is a start up Institute in the field of Cyber Security and Ethical Hacking training. We have started from Vadodara city and about to expand all over Gujarat and Nation Wide. Inferno InfoSec Institute is motivating and guiding students towards the field of Cyber Security and Ethical Hacking. Knowing the current trends about Cyber Security & Ethical Hacking, there is a huge career scope for the students in this field.
Our training and module is designed in such a manner that it helps any student to get hands on practical knowledge in the field of IT security. One can learn, grow and Evolve himself/ herself in the IT security field with Inferno InfoSec. Due to our focus on practical based knowledge it helps out the students to face the corporate IT security challenges and get them solved with their highly developed skills.
